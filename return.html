Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not satisfied, you can return the product within 10 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange.

Conditions for Returns

  • The product must be unused and in the same condition that you received it.
  • The product must be in the original packaging.
  • You must have the receipt or proof of purchase.

Non-returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned, including:

  • Gift cards
  • Downloadable software products
  • Health and personal care items

Return Process

To return a product, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team to initiate the return process.
  2. Pack the product securely in its original packaging
  3. include the receipt or proof of purchase,along with any accessories or iteams that came with the product
  4. send the package to the Address provide by our customer service team
